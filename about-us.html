About Twin Tech India

Leading manufacturer of aerosols, sprays, and specialty industrial products. Committed to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction since our inception.

Our Story

Company Overview

TWIN TECH INDIA PVT. LTD

Aerosol Spray Manufacturer

Twin Tech India Pvt. Ltd is a leading name in manufacturing and supplying of aerosols and sprays in a multitude of industrial application. Biodegradable, user & environment friendly, all our sprays are custom designed for easy use. We promote our aerosols for different applications under the registered brand name of SPRAYZET.

We also do contract aerosol packaging for a variety of companies and industries, with extensive private label aerosol filling / packaging capabilities.

"We have earned a good reputation in the industry through our committed and outstanding customer management system and have created a benchmark as a manufacturer of various industrial aerosols."

"Twin Tech" is the manufacturer, exporter & supplier of specialty industrial maintenance aerosols like On-Line Contact Cleaner, Carbon Remover, Electronic Cleaner, Insulating Spray Varnish, Bus-Bar Coatings, Penetrating Oil, Moisture Displacer, Rust Preservatives, Rust-Remover, Anti-Seize Compound, Silicon-Free Mould Release Agent, Mould & Die Protector, Mould Cleaner, Galvanizing Coating, etc.

Twin Tech India Pvt. Ltd is also manufacturing various Aerosol machines like Aerosol propellant Filling Machine, Aerosol Semi-Automatic Crimping Machine, Aerosol Crimping Machine (Bottle), Aerosol Valve's Deep Tube Cutting Machine etc. The company has been supplying these machines in India and abroad, with a sound record of trouble free operations. The machines supplied are of a very high quality and are low on maintenance. It requires minimum degree of operating skills to work on these machines, which helps the customers to cut down the costs of employing skilled labor.

Twin Tech India Pvt. Ltd is also manufacturing self adhesive tapes, home care products eg: Mosquito Repellent under brand name "Hacker", Home Cleaning Products, "Twinzy" Air Deo, Car Care products under brand name "Evershine".

We have been capable to meet the demands of different industries with our new technology, consistent production method with the most efficient manner. Our products are appropriate for clients based across agriculture, plastic, engineering, textile, food manufacturing, construction and automotive industries. We have been able to meet the bulk demands in well-organized method.

"While maintaining a high level of quality in its products, the company also ensures a timely delivery of the machines to all its clients, which has helped the company to earn the goodwill of its customers."

With a vast experience in Industry, the reputation we have achieved throughout the years has been the result of our hard work and unwavering commitment to providing complete customer satisfaction with maintaining quality standard in the products.

Industries We Serve

Application Areas

Twin Tech India Pvt Ltd, based in Ghaziabad, is a manufacturer and supplier of Aerosol cans and sprays for variety of industrial application with endless possibilities and no boundaries. No matter what the application is, our products have been using in almost every industries and giving good results with customer satisfaction.

Process Industry

Twin Tech India offers customers a wide range of maintenance aerosols for process industries like On-line electrical contact cleaners, Moisture Displacer, Carbon Remover, Contact Lube. Dry Lubrication, Spray Lubricants, Rust Remover, insulating & protective coatings, HT tapes and many more…

Automotive Industry

In the automotive sector, Twin Tech India offers customers an extensive range of aerosols, lubricants and maintenance products. In general, Corrosion Inhibitors, Rust penetrator, Cleaning & Degreasing of machinery parts, engines, bearings, metal molds, moisture proofing, lubrication in extreme temperature and many more…

Telecommunication Industry

We offers customers a wide range of cleaning and degreasers for safely and efficiently cleaning and removing grease, oxides, condensation, dust, grime and other contaminants from optical fiber cables, connectors used in telecommunication. Our product has been also used in radio antenna assemblies for reducing contact resistance.

Electrical & Electronics Industry

A wide range of products line On-line Electrical Cleaner, Electronic Cleaner, PCB Coatings, Tuff Clean, Cold Galvanizing Compounds, Anti-Track Coatings, HT Tapes for 11KV and 33KV Insulations. Our products are recommended for cleaning electric motor windings, cables, electronic equipments etc and also PCB coating enhances the working lifespan and reliability of electronic PCB assemblies.

IT Industry

Twin Tech India offers customers a wide range of cleaning and degreasers for safely removing dust and dirt without contaminating either your computer, or the environment. Our product is used hundreds of times every day by technicians to keep computers and data centers clean.

Textile Industry

Twin Tech India offers a wide range of Dry Lubricants and anti seize spray that has been using in textile equipments for long lasting drip free lubrication like Dry Graphite lube, PTFE Dry Film Lubricant, Dry Moly Lubricant and Copper/Moly based antiseize compounds.

Pharmaceuticals Industry

Twin Tech India offers a wide range of cleaning products which synthetic surface active compound exceptionally effective for cleaning various kinds of contaminants i:e oils, greases, carbon, dirt, grime etc. it is non-toxic biodegradable product, immediately soluble in water. It is very useful for cleaning of floors & machines agent, vessels and containers in pharmaceuticals industries.

Other Application Areas

Twin Tech India is also offering a wide range of home care products eg: glass cleaner, furniture polish, room freshener, air deo, shoe deo, helmet deo etc and Car care products eg: Car wash shampoo, Tyre and Dashboard shiner, All in one rust remover, brake cleaner, car perfume, air dry spray paint, car wax and many more…

Twin Tech India is also offering a wide range of adhesive tapes for floor line marking, sealing of ducts, bundling and stripling of heavy goods, holding of condenser coils in refrigerators, PE surface protection, masking tape, packaging, labeling, electrical insulation, double layer advertising tape and many more…

Twin tech India is also engaged in Contract packaging for a number of companies and industries, with extensive private label aerosol filling / packaging capabilities.
